Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

The short version: Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it. We cannot see, read, access, or sell your documents. There are no FlyID servers storing your personal information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

FlyID is designed as an offline-first application.

  • Local storage only: All document data — including scanned text and biometric photos — is stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's built-in secure storage.
  • Device encryption: The data stored by FlyID is protected by iOS's sandbox encryption and cannot be accessed by other apps. You can lock access to the app behind Face ID or Touch ID.
  • No account required: You don't need to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use FlyID. There is no central FlyID database.

2. Camera and Chip Usage

FlyID uses specific hardware solely to help you digitize your documents.

  • Camera: Used strictly for on-device reading of the printed code on your documents. Image frames are processed in real-time and immediately discarded once data is extracted.
  • NFC chip reader: Used to communicate with the cryptographic chip in modern e-Passports. Data read from the chip (biometric photo, verified details) goes directly to the app's local secure storage.

3. Encrypted Sharing

If you use Emergency Sharing, your chosen document is packaged and encrypted on your device before transmission. The link is time-limited. We facilitate the encrypted transfer but never hold the decryption keys — we cannot read what you share.

4. Analytics and Crash Reports

FlyID does not include third-party tracking tools. We only receive standard, anonymous crash reports through Apple's built-in App Store analytics, and only if you have opted in via your iOS settings. These reports contain technical logs to help fix bugs and never contain any personal document data.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store and managed via RevenueCat, a trusted subscription platform. RevenueCat receives only anonymous transaction identifiers from Apple. We do not collect or have access to your credit card information, billing address, or payment history.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@flyid.app.
