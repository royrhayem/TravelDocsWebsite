Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

At FlyID, your privacy is not just a feature; it is the entire foundation of our application. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to keeping your sensitive travel documents mathematically secure and entirely out of our hands.

1. The Short Version

Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it. We cannot see, read, access, or sell your passports or visas. We have no external servers storing your personal information.

2. Data Collection and Storage (Offline-First)

FlyID is designed as an "offline-first" application.

  • Local Storage: All document data, including scanned text and high-resolution biometric photos, are stored exclusively on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's built-in secure storage.
  • Device Encryption: The data stored by FlyID is protected by iOS's built-in sandbox encryption and cannot be accessed by other apps. You can (and should) strictly lock access to the app behind Face ID or Touch ID.
  • No Cloud Accounts: You are not required to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use FlyID. There is no central FlyID database.

3. Camera and NFC Usage

FlyID uses specific hardware components solely for the purpose of helping you digitize your documents:

  • Camera Access: The camera is used strictly for on-device reading of the printed code on your documents. The actual image frames are processed in real-time and immediately discarded once the data is extracted.
  • NFC Reader Access: The NFC reader is used to securely communicate with the cryptographic chip embedded in modern e-Passports. The data read from the chip (such as your biometric photo and verified demographic details) is transferred directly to the app's secure local secure local storage.

4. Encrypted Sharing

If you choose to use the "Emergency Sharing" feature, your chosen document is packaged, encrypted on your device, and transmitted using end-to-end encryption. The link is time-limited. We facilitate the encrypted transfer but never possess the decryption keys required to read the shared document.

5. Analytics and Crash Reports

FlyID does not include third-party tracking SDKs (like Facebook Pixel or Google Analytics) that monitor your behavior. We only receive standard, anonymous crash reports provided entirely through Apple’s built-in App Store analytics, and only if you have explicitly opted-in via your iOS settings. These crash reports contain technical logs to help us fix bugs and never contain any personal document data.

6. Subscriptions and Payments

All purchases and subscriptions for FlyID Pro are processed through Apple's App Store and managed via RevenueCat, a trusted third-party subscription management platform. RevenueCat receives only anonymous transaction identifiers from Apple to manage your subscription status. We do not collect, process, or have access to your credit card information, billing address, or payment history.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the App or by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:
