Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe if I lose my phone?

Yes. FlyID stores your data entirely on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework, protected by iOS sandbox encryption. Furthermore, access to the app itself is strictly locked behind Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode. Without your biometrics or passcode, your data cannot be accessed by anyone who finds your phone.

Does FlyID scan my biometric photo?

Yes! When you scan the securely encrypted NFC chip inside modern e-Passports, FlyID extracts the high-resolution facial image stored on the document's chip and saves it locally in your app vault. This data is never sent to our servers.

Why is the NFC scan failing on my passport?

NFC reading can sometimes be sensitive to positioning. Try the following:

  • Remove any thick cases from your iPhone.
  • Place the top edge of your iPhone near the bottom half of the passport's back cover, or open the passport to the data page and place your phone over it.
  • Hold your phone completely still for a few seconds when the prompt appears.
How do I manage or cancel my FlyID Pro subscription?

Because all billing is handled securely by Apple, you must manage your subscription through the App Store.

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  • Tap your name at the top.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select FlyID Pro and choose "Cancel Subscription".
