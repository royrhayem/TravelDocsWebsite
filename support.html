How can we help?

Answers to the most common questions about FlyID.

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe if I lose my phone? +

Yes. FlyID stores your data entirely on your device, protected by iOS encryption. Access to the app is locked behind Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode — without your biometrics or passcode, nobody can get in.

Does FlyID upload anything to a server? +

No. FlyID is offline-first. Your documents, photos, and personal data never leave your device unless you explicitly use the Emergency Sharing feature — and even then the data is end-to-end encrypted and the link is time-limited.

Scanning

Does FlyID scan my biometric photo? +

Yes. When you scan the chip inside a modern e-Passport, FlyID extracts the high-resolution facial image stored on it and saves it locally. This data is never sent to our servers.

Why is the chip scan failing on my passport? +

Chip reading can be sensitive to positioning. A few things to try:

  • Remove any thick case from your iPhone.
  • Place the top edge of your iPhone near the bottom half of the passport's back cover, or open the passport to the data page and rest your phone on it.
  • Hold your phone completely still for a few seconds when the prompt appears.

Subscription

How do I manage or cancel FlyID Pro? +

All billing is handled by Apple. To manage your subscription:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone.
  • Tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions.
  • Select FlyID Pro and choose "Cancel Subscription".
